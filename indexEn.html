A piece of history

In the heart of the oldest city in Europe, a portal is waiting for you. A portal that offers a closer look into the past and an opportunity to celebrate the present. Located in the center of Plovdiv, within a walking distance of the main cultural and tourist attractions, Villa Flavia Boutique Hotel offers you a unique atmosphere and exciting experiences.


The hotel building has the character of the typical Plovdiv architecture from the beginning of the 20th century, wrapped in the French secession.


The uniqueness of the hotel is complemented by its authentic spirit and attitude to detail. The stylishly furnished rooms and suites offer a charming (harmonious – в българската версия на текста използваме „хармоничен“, но charming излъчва по-положителни емоции и се асоциира с нещо хубаво) mixture of classics and modernism, and the combination of modern amenities and unadulterated luxury will make Villa Flavia Boutique Hotel your new favorite place (spot – звучи по изразително, но означава и „петно“ за това по-добре да не го използваме) to relax and meet with history.

Survived to be seen

Remains of a Roman bath dating from the 1st-2nd century have been found in the foundations of the hotel. The remains are well preserved and exhibited and provide an exceptional view in the heart of the majestic and millennial city of Plovdiv.


Villa Flavia is not just a hotel - it is the home of history, culture and exquisite design that will share with you unforgettable feelings and centuries of history.
