The history behind

The unique Villa Flavia boutique hotel is a real jewel in the heart of Plovdiv, the oldest city in Europe.
The hotel is a reconstruction of a traditional Plovdiv old town house by arch. Djakov from the 1920s.

Survived to be seen

During the reconstruction, the architects find in the basement of the house, the remains of a Roman bath, dating back almost 2,000 years. Lying on the ruins of time, Villa Flavia will offer you an unforgettable ancient experience in the unadulterated spirit of Rome.
